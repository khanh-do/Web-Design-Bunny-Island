VISITOR GUIDELINES

If you don’t like super cute things, don’t make a trip to Bunny Island.  But if you want to risk it anyway, beware of a few guidelines to minimize your visiting pleasure. Don’t make any sound of rabbit food shaking in a bag if you don’t want to create a bunny stampede heading in your direction. They will hop onto your lap, climb into your arms, or totally cover you in bunny love if you let them.
